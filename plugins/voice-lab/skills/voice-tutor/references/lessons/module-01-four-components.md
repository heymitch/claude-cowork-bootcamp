# Module 1: The 4 Components of Your Writing Voice

To understand how to isolate your writing voice, it's important to realize that your voice is made up of four key elements:

1. Voice
2. Tone
3. Style
4. Structure

Your unique writing personality exists at the intersection of these four factors.

The cool thing is you can create any voice you'd like by consciously mixing and matching different identifiable attributes together—no different than the way a painter mixes colors on a palette.

- Word choice
- Cadence and rhythm
- Even the decision to use (or not use) data to back up an opinion.

## A voice is just a combination of identifiable attributes.

While we'll focus primarily on Voice and Style for AI voice cloning, we'll touch on aspects of Tone and Structure as well, as all four elements contribute to your unique writing personality.

By the end of this module, you'll have a comprehensive understanding of what makes your writing uniquely yours, and how to instruct AI to replicate it.

## Here's the simple process we'll follow to clone your voice:

1. Gather 3-5 samples of your best writing
2. Run the samples through our Voice DNA prompts (Voice Extraction and Archetype Analysis)
3. Analyze your writing style and quirks
4. Compile your consolidated Voice and Style attributes
5. Create a customized AI instruction template

Once you have your Voice DNA and custom instruction template, you'll be ready to start generating highly personalized content with AI that captures your voice.

By teaching AI to mimic your voice, you can generate first drafts with fewer edits.
