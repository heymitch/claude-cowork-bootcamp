# Platform Setup Guides

Quick-start guides for the three best platforms to sell your first digital product.

## Recommendation

If this is your first digital product, use Gumroad. It is the fastest path from zero to live sale. You can switch platforms later when you outgrow it.

## Gumroad

### Setup (15-20 minutes)
1. Go to gumroad.com and create a free account
2. Click "New Product" in your dashboard
3. Select product type: "Digital product"
4. Upload your file (PDF, ZIP, etc.)
5. Set your price
6. Add a cover image (1280x720px works best)
7. Write your product description (use the template from ltl-frameworks.md)
8. Add preview pages (first 3-5 pages of your product — lets buyers peek inside)
9. Click "Publish"
10. Copy your product link and share it

### Features
- Built-in email list (collect emails from buyers and free subscribers)
- Affiliate program (let others promote your product for a commission)
- Discount codes (create launch specials or bundle deals)
- Analytics dashboard (see where buyers come from)
- Custom permalink (gumroad.com/l/your-product-name)
- Automatic file delivery after purchase

### Fees
10% flat on every sale. No monthly fee.

On a $29 product: $2.90 fee, you keep $26.10.

### Best For
First-time sellers, digital products under $50, quick setup, people who want everything in one place.

### Tips
- Customize your permalink — gumroad.com/l/product-name looks better than a random string
- Upload 2-3 preview pages so buyers can see the quality before purchasing
- Customize your email receipt — the default is plain, add a personal thank-you message
- Use the "Pay what you want" option with a minimum price to increase average order value
- Enable the "offer code" field on checkout to run promotions

## Stripe + Payment Links

### Setup (20-30 minutes)
1. Go to stripe.com and create an account (you will need to verify your identity)
2. In your dashboard, go to "Products" and click "+ Add product"
3. Enter product name, description, and price
4. Click "Create payment link" on the product page
5. Customize the checkout page (add your logo, product image, description)
6. Copy the payment link
7. Share the link — customers click it, pay, and you get the money

### Delivering the Product
Stripe does not host files. After purchase, you need to deliver the product yourself:
- **Option A:** Set up a "success URL" that redirects to a download page you host
- **Option B:** Use Stripe's webhook to trigger an automatic email with the download link
- **Option C:** Manually email the file to buyers (fine for your first 10-20 sales)

### Features
- Professional checkout page (customizable)
- Subscriptions and one-time payments
- Full payment processing with global support
- Webhooks for automation
- Detailed financial reporting

### Fees
2.9% + $0.30 per transaction. No monthly fee.

On a $29 product: $1.14 fee, you keep $27.86.

### Best For
Existing businesses, professional checkout experience, people who want lower fees and are willing to handle delivery separately.

### Tips
- Use Payment Links for simplicity — no code needed
- Add your logo and brand colors to the checkout page
- Set up email notifications so you know when someone buys
- If you outgrow manual delivery, add a simple webhook to automate it

## Lemon Squeezy

### Setup (15-20 minutes)
1. Go to lemonsqueezy.com and create an account
2. Create your "Store" (this is your storefront)
3. Click "New Product"
4. Upload your file
5. Set pricing (one-time or subscription)
6. Add cover image and description
7. Publish
8. Share your product link

### Features
- Handles global sales tax automatically (VAT, GST — this is a big deal for international sales)
- Built-in email marketing
- License key generation (great for software)
- Affiliate program
- Custom checkout overlay (embeds on your site)
- Automatic file delivery

### Fees
5% + $0.50 per transaction. No monthly fee.

On a $29 product: $1.95 fee, you keep $27.05.

### Best For
International sellers who do not want to deal with tax compliance, SaaS or software products, people who want Gumroad-like simplicity with better tax handling.

### Tips
- The automatic sales tax handling alone is worth using this platform if you sell internationally
- Use the checkout overlay to embed purchasing directly on your website
- Set up the affiliate program early — let your buyers become promoters

## Common Mistakes

1. **Spending days picking a platform.** Pick one, set it up, move on. You can switch later.
2. **Not testing checkout.** Buy your own product. Use a 100% discount code or test mode. Make sure the file downloads correctly.
3. **Skipping the product description.** A blank product page with just a price will not sell. Write a real description.
4. **Ignoring mobile checkout.** Most people will find your product on their phone. Test the purchase flow on mobile.
5. **Forgetting about delivery.** On Stripe, you have to deliver the file yourself. Set this up before you launch, not after your first confused buyer emails you.
